Member Benefits and Events

As a member, you'll have access to these benefits:

  • National JACL Pacific Citizen newspaper bimonthly
  • Arizona JACL Roundup newsletter monthly
  • Local and National scholarship opportunities
  • JACL Credit Union Services

In addition, the Arizona chapter offers many events throughout the year such as:

  • Annual Picnic
  • Annual summer bowling league
  • Annual scholarship luncheon
  • Monthly women's activity group
  • Monthly board meeting (all members may attend)
  • and more!
